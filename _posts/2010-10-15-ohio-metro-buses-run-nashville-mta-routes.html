For the last several months I kept seeing all these Ohio Metro buses running through Nashville. I just assumed Nashville MTA had bought a bunch of used buses and hadn't gotten around to repainting them (much less getting new tags).

As It turns out, Cincinnati has loaned 25 buses to Nashville MTA to fill the gap from Nashville buses lost to the historic flood back in May.

A fine gesture of goodwill from Ohio.
