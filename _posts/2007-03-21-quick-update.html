Just a few things that have been standing out the last week or two.

Twitter just keeps building and building. TwitterVision pretty much sums it all up.

Jimmy just pointed me toward UbuntuStudio due in April. This is something that is probably great for enthusiasts and people who have never touched an image, audio or video manipulation application. But for anyone who is familiar with programs like Photoshop, After Effects or any of the pro video apps that Avid or Apple offer, this is not going to be usable. 10 minutes with the Gimp is enough to say, “no thanks.”

AppleTV is now shipping. You should be able to check it out at your local Apple Store this weekend. This has the potential to be iPod big.
