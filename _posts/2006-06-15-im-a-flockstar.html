I'm not diggin' the "Flockstar" marketing idea(sounds very 13-year-old MTV crowd), but Flock is quickly becoming my browser of choice. With built-in blogging, Flickr and RSS tools, they have the potential to eliminate the need for separate apps like MarsEdit, Flickr Uploader, and NewsFire.
