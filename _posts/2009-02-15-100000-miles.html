I've officially put 100,000 miles on the Jetta since I've owned it. It took 5.5 years @ roughly 18,000 miles per year.

The car is actually running better now than it ever has, so I'm looking forward to many many more miles with it.

Here's a list of issues and repairs during the last 100,000 miles:

  • Throttlebody replacement
  • Timing belt replacement
  • 2 catalytic converter replacements
  • 2 sets of tires
  • Several reattachments of the front underneith splash guard
  • Water pump replacement
  • Soon to be 2 sets of floor mats

Probably couldn't ask for much less.
