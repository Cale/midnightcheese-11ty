I was lucky enough to attend Bonnaroo with my Dad for the day on Saturday. What an amazing experience. 80,000 people all in one place all attending to have a good time and listen to some great music. No conflicts, no aggression. Everyone was equal.

So much great music all weekend. I was able to see Beck, Radiohead, and Dr. John on Saturday. Radiohead was amazing. They played for well over 2 hours. Thom Yorke was exceptionally graceful not saying much more than a quiet "thank you" after each song.
