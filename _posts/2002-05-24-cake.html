Ahhh... tonight was the first Dancin' in the District of the season. It was quite the fantastic show. Cake and Superdrag did a great job. I think Cake got a little carried away with trying to get the crowd to sing along... the show was a little late getting started... none of the stage lights worked for half the show, and Nashville burned down in the background as the bands played in the dark, but all in all, the music was good and that's what counts.
