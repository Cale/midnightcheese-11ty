First off, Kubrick is beautiful. It's great. The colors, the fonts, the minimalism of it all, I just love. Kubrick is so good in fact, that I'm almost scared to see what happens now that it's the default theme in WordPress 1.5. I'm worried that it's going to become so popular and abundant that it's going to become commonplace, dull, and no longer visually enticing. Maybe this is all just crazy-talk. I just don't want to see a good design go bad.
